This game needs 4 game controllers(XBox or equivalent)
Tutorial will teach you the basic controls, but I will list them here for reference
************************************************************************************
Snowman Character:
X: Dash
B: Build a snow wall
A: Collect snow from the walls you built
RB: Melee attack
-------------------------------------------
Snowboy Character
A: Collect Snow from snow piles or your snowman teammate
B: Build cannon on top of the snow wall built by teammate snowman
RB: aim & shoot
once the cannon is built: press B to get in or get out of the cannon, 
RB to shoot gaint cannon balls, Right Stick to control the cannon's aim.
*************************************************************************************
Left Stick & Right Stick to move around & navigate your character
